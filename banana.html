Banana Bread

Baking Time: 50 minutes

Ingredients









Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C.
  2. Grease a 10cm x 24cm (base measurement) loaf pan and line the base and 2 long sides with baking paper, allowing the sides to overhang.
  3. Combine the flour, baking powder and sugar in a large bowl.
  4. Whisk the egg, oil, buttermilk and mashed banana in a large jug.
  5. Add to the flour mixture and stir to combine.
  6. Spoon into the prepared pan and smooth the surface.
  7. Bake for 45 minutes or until skewer inserted in centre comes out clean.
  8. Set aside in the pan for 10 minutes to cool slightly before transferring to a wire rack. Cut into slices and serve warm or at room temperature.
