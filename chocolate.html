Chocolate Chip Cookies

Baking Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients









Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 160Â°C and line 2 baking trays with baking paper.
  2. Using an electric mixer or whisking by hand, beat butter, brown sugar and caster sugar for 1-2 minutes or until smooth and well combined.
  3. Beat in egg and vanilla until combined.
  4. Stir in flour then both choc chips.
  5. Roll 2 level tablespoons of mixture into balls and place on prepared tray, 3cm apart.
  6. Bake for 15-18 minutes or until light golden and cooked.
  7. Transfer to a wire rack to cool.
