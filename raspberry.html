White Chocolate Raspberry Muffins

Baking Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients












Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C and grease a muffin tin with butter or spray oil.
  2. Sift flour into a large bowl and stir in the sugars to mix evenly.
  3. In another bowl mix together the egg, lemon zest, butter, buttermilk and choc chips and add flour mixture with the raspberries.
  4. Spoon into muffin tin and bake for 20 minutes or until golden.
  5. Remove from oven and allow to cool for a few minutes before turning out onto a wire rack to cool completely.
