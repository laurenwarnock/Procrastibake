Caramel Slice

Baking Time: One hour

Steps

Base

  1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C.
  2. Sift flour into bowl, add sugar and coconut, stir until combined.
  3. Melt butter, add dry to ingredients; mix well.
  4. Press into greased 28cm x 18cm baking pan.
  5. Bake for 10 minutes.

Filling

  1. Combine condensed milk, butter and golden syrup in a medium saucepan.
  2. Stir over a low heat until butter is melted.
  3. Increase to medium heat and stir constantly for about 8 to 10 minutes, or until mixture boils and turns light golden.
  4. Pour hot filling over biscuit base and return to oven for 10 minutes.
  5. Melt chocolate and pour over filling. Sprinkle with salt.
  6. Refrigerate for at least 6 hours.
